# Source Map — Trusted Sources Per Research Dimension

## Market Research Sources

### Pricing & Transaction Data
- **Edmunds.com** — True Market Value (TMV), dealer invoice, transaction prices, incentives
- **KBB.com** — Fair Purchase Price, 5-Year Cost to Own, trade-in values
- **TrueCar** — Average paid prices by region, dealer pricing transparency
- **Cars.com** — Listing prices, inventory levels, market trends
- **CarGurus** — Deal ratings, price history, instant market value

### Depreciation & Resale
- **iSeeCars.com** — Depreciation studies, best/worst resale value rankings
- **KBB Residual Values** — Predicted residual value percentages
- **Edmunds TCO** — 5-year depreciation projections

### Incentives & Deals
- **Manufacturer websites** — Current rebates, APR offers, lease specials
- **CarsDirect** — Monthly deal roundups, best incentives by brand
- **Edmunds Deals** — Editor-curated best current deals

## Reliability Sources

### Ratings & Studies
- **Consumer Reports** — Predicted reliability, owner satisfaction, road test scores
- **JD Power** — Vehicle Dependability Study (VDS), Initial Quality Study (IQS)
- **NHTSA** — Recall database, complaints, investigations, safety ratings (5-star)
- **IIHS** — Crash test ratings, Top Safety Pick / Top Safety Pick+

### Owner Experiences
- **CarComplaints.com** — Owner-reported problems by model year, "Beware of the Clunker" alerts
- **TrueDelta** — Repair frequency data from owner surveys
- **Reddit** — r/whatcarshouldIbuy, r/askcarsales, r/cars, r/mechanicadvice
- **Edmunds Owner Reviews** — Long-term owner ratings and reviews

### Recall & Safety
- **NHTSA Recalls** — recalls.gov, full recall database with completion rates
- **NHTSA Complaints** — Owner-submitted complaints, investigation status
- **IIHS Crash Tests** — Frontal, side, rollover, pedestrian, headlight ratings

## Cost of Ownership Sources

### Insurance
- **Insure.com** — Average insurance costs by make/model
- **NerdWallet** — Insurance comparison guides, average rates
- **The Zebra** — Insurance cost data by vehicle
- **IIHS/HLDI** — Insurance loss data, theft rates, injury claim rates

### Fuel Economy
- **FuelEconomy.gov** — EPA MPG ratings (official), annual fuel cost estimates
- **Edmunds Real-World MPG** — Community-reported actual fuel economy
- **Car and Driver Testing** — Tested highway MPG

### Maintenance & Repair
- **Edmunds True Cost to Own** — 5-year maintenance + repair cost projections
- **RepairPal** — Average repair costs by vehicle, maintenance schedules
- **YourMechanic** — Maintenance cost studies by brand
- **Manufacturer Service Schedules** — Official recommended service intervals

## Feature & Review Sources

### Expert Reviews
- **Car and Driver** — In-depth reviews, comparison tests, 10Best lists
- **MotorTrend** — First drives, comparisons, Car of the Year
- **Edmunds Expert Reviews** — Pro/con summaries, trim recommendations
- **Kelley Blue Book Reviews** — Expert ratings, best buy awards

### Specifications & Comparisons
- **Manufacturer Websites** — Official specs, trim configurators, build-and-price
- **Cars.com Compare** — Side-by-side spec comparisons
- **Edmunds Compare** — Feature-by-feature trim comparison
- **CarFax** — Vehicle history for used/CPO options

### Safety Ratings
- **IIHS** — crashtest results, Top Safety Pick lists (primary)
- **NHTSA** — 5-star safety ratings, rollover risk (primary)
- **Euro NCAP** — Additional safety data for shared platforms

## Search Query Patterns

### Market Research
- `"[make model year]" price OR MSRP OR "transaction price" site:edmunds.com`
- `"[make model]" incentives OR rebates [month] 2026`
- `"[make model]" depreciation OR "resale value" iSeeCars OR kbb`

### Reliability
- `"[make model]" reliability rating consumer reports 2025 OR 2026`
- `site:nhtsa.gov "[make model year]" recalls`
- `site:carcomplaints.com "[make model]" problems`

### Cost
- `"[make model]" "true cost to own" OR TCO site:edmunds.com`
- `"[make model]" insurance cost average annual`
- `"[make model]" MPG fuel economy site:fueleconomy.gov`

### Features
- `"[make model year]" trim comparison features specifications`
- `"[make model]" safety rating IIHS OR NHTSA`
- `"[make model]" vs "[competitor]" comparison`
