# Medigap Research Methodology

Research methodology for comparing Medigap Plan G vs Plan N across Maryland (21401) and Pennsylvania (15401).

## Reference Documents

Load these on-demand when conducting medigap research:

- `references/plan-g-vs-n-matrix.md` — Coverage comparison, deductibles, excess charges
- `references/source-map.md` — Trusted sources by research dimension

## Research Dimensions

1. **Premium Data**: Current Plan G and Plan N premiums by zip code from 8-10 insurers
2. **Insurer Quality**: AM Best ratings, NAIC complaint ratios, rate increase history
3. **State Rules**: Birthday rule (MD), switching rules, open enrollment, guaranteed issue
4. **Claims Patterns**: Break-even visit threshold, excess charge prevalence, projected costs

## When to Use

- When conducting Medigap plan research for the selector pipeline
- When the user asks about Medicare supplement plans
- When comparing Plan G vs Plan N coverage or costs
