✅ 1. Validated Consensus

These insights carry highest confidence — independent sources converged on the same conclusions.

Commenting Is a Growth Engine

Sanders: 30/30/10 allocation across audience tiers. Firth: Commenting signals expertise to interest-based algorithm (confirmed by 360Brew AI). Priestley: Lurker engagement — 99% of audience watches but never engages.

Synthesis: Strategic commenting is a distribution mechanism equal to or greater than posting itself.

Profile = Conversion Asset

Sanders: "Profile as Landing Page" with headshot, CTA banner, featured, origin story. Firth: Featured section as lead gen real estate. Priestley: Profile must support the CTA chain.

Synthesis: The profile is not a resume. Featured section is unanimously underutilized prime real estate.

Content Must Serve a Funnel

Sanders: Explicit TOF/MOF/BOF framework. Firth: Implied via Events + Featured. Priestley: CTA types map to funnel stages.

Synthesis: Every piece of content should have a known position in the funnel. Random posting = wasted effort.

Contextual CTAs Win (2/3 agree, 1 contradicted)

Sanders + Firth: Contextual, integrated CTAs. Priestley: "By the way" unrelated CTAs — CONTRADICTED by LinkedIn guidance and higher-credibility sources.

Synthesis: ContentPulse should enforce contextual CTA matching.

🎯 4. Unified LinkedIn Strategy

WEEKS 1-2

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Professional headshot + CTA banner image with clear value proposition
  • Featured section: lead magnets, top posts, or upcoming Event
  • About section as origin story (Sanders) with contextual CTA
  • Headline as value proposition, not job title
  • Define 3-4 Expert Pillars + 2-3 Interest Pillars
  • Cross-reference pillars with 3S+2F angles to build content matrix
  • Start 30/30/10 commenting: 10-15 thoughtful comments/day
WEEKS 3-4

Phase 2: Content Engine

  • Hook: L1 ~65 chars, L2 ~40 chars (Sanders anatomy)
  • Body: Expansion → Bulk → Dream Destination → CTA
  • Pre-validated content approach: find outlier posts, improve them
  • Contextual CTAs only — no "by the way" disconnected CTAs
  • NO links in post body (use first-comment or bio link)
  • 3-4 posts/week mixing TOF, MOF, and BOF content
  • Track reposts > saves > sends as primary metrics
WEEKS 5+

Phase 3: Lead Generation

  • Lead magnet aligned to pillars (assessment/quiz)
  • Newsletter for deplatforming (own your audience)
  • LinkedIn Events for high-intent leads (Firth)
  • Webinars for conversion (Sanders)
  • Segmented email sequences based on quiz/assessment responses
  • Blank connection requests to active commenters
  • "With or without you" CTA energy — never desperate
