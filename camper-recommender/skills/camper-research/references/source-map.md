# Source Map — Trusted Sources Per Research Dimension (RV/Camper)

## Market Research Sources

### Pricing & Transaction Data
- **RVTrader.com** — Largest RV marketplace, listing prices, inventory levels, market trends
- **NADA Guides (RV edition)** — Authoritative RV values (the KBB equivalent for RVs), used/trade-in/retail values
- **Camping World / General RV** — Major dealer chains, new/used pricing, show specials
- **PPL Motor Homes** — Consignment pricing, sold listing data, market value reference
- **RVUSA.com** — Dealer listings, inventory search, price comparison

### Depreciation & Resale
- **NADA Guides RV** — Historical and projected residual values by make/model/year
- **RVTrader Market Data** — Listing price trends over time
- **JD Power RV Values** — Depreciation projections and value retention by brand
- **PPL Sold Listings** — Actual sale prices for consignment RVs

### Incentives & Deals
- **Manufacturer websites** — Current rebates, show specials, financing offers
- **Camping World Sales Events** — Major sale events, clearance pricing
- **RV show schedules** — Jan-Mar show season typically has best deals ($3K-$8K savings)
- **Dealer end-of-season clearance** — Sep-Oct clearance on current model year

## Reliability Sources

### Build Quality & Ratings
- **RV Insider** — Professional ratings, build quality assessments, manufacturer tier rankings
- **RV Consumer Group** — Independent ratings (subscription-based), quality scores
- **RVIA (RV Industry Association)** — Industry data, certified manufacturers

### Owner Experiences & Forums
- **iRV2.com** — Largest RV forum, long-term owner reviews, problem reports
- **Forest River Forums** — Brand-specific owner community (Forest River, Coachmen, etc.)
- **Grand Design Owners Forum** — Brand-specific community, known for engaged ownership
- **Reddit** — r/RVLiving, r/GoRVing, r/TravelTrailers, r/FullTimeRV
- **YouTube Reviews** — Keep Your Daydream, Do It Yourself RV, All About RV's, Changing Lanes

### Common Issues & Defects
- **RV Recalls** — NHTSA recall database (same as vehicles)
- **Forum search** — "[make model] problems" on iRV2, brand forums
- **YouTube walkaround reviews** — Often catch quality issues in detail

## Cost of Ownership Sources

### Insurance
- **Good Sam Insurance** — Specialty RV insurance, replacement cost coverage
- **Progressive RV** — Specialty RV policies, full-timer coverage
- **National General** — RV insurance with agreed value options
- **Escapees RV Club** — Insurance comparison tools, member discounts

### Storage
- **SpareFoot / Neighbor** — Storage unit price comparison by region
- **Dealer storage programs** — Some dealers offer storage for purchases
- **Regional averages** — Outdoor $75-$125/mo, covered $150-$250/mo, indoor $200-$400/mo

### Campground & Travel
- **KOA.com** — Campground rates, annual membership ($33/year for 10% discount)
- **Thousand Trails** — Membership camping, zone passes
- **Reserve America / Recreation.gov** — State and national park campground rates
- **Campendium** — User-reported rates, boondocking locations
- **Escapees RV Club** — Discounted rates, mail forwarding for full-timers

### Maintenance
- **Manufacturer service schedules** — Recommended maintenance intervals
- **Mobile RV service providers** — Typical rates for common repairs
- **RV winterization guides** — Annual costs for cold-weather owners
- **Roof maintenance** — Resealing costs ($200-$500/year DIY, $500-$1,500 professional)

## Feature & Review Sources

### Expert Reviews
- **Do It Yourself RV** — Detailed walkthroughs, floorplan analysis, feature reviews
- **Keep Your Daydream** — Long-term RV reviews, brand comparisons
- **RV Insider** — Professional reviews, floorplan analysis
- **All About RV's** — Tech-focused reviews, weight analysis

### Specifications & Comparisons
- **Manufacturer websites** — Official specs, floorplan diagrams, build-and-price tools
- **RVTrader Compare** — Side-by-side spec comparisons
- **RV Insider Compare** — Feature-by-feature comparison tools
- **Tow capacity calculators** — Match GVWR/hitch weight to tow vehicle ratings

### Safety & Towing
- **Manufacturer tow guides** — Ford, Ram, Chevy tow rating lookup tools
- **SAE J2807** — Towing standard compliance verification
- **NHTSA** — RV-specific safety recalls and investigations

## Search Query Patterns

### Market Research
- `"[make model floorplan]" MSRP OR price OR "dealer price" site:rvtrader.com`
- `"[make model]" incentives OR rebates OR "show special" 2026`
- `"[rv type]" depreciation OR "resale value" brand comparison NADA`

### Reliability
- `"[make model]" build quality OR reliability OR review site:irv2.com`
- `"[make]" manufacturer reputation OR quality tier OR ranking`
- `"[make model]" problems OR issues OR defects roof OR water OR slide`

### Cost
- `"[rv type]" insurance cost OR rate average annual specialty RV`
- `"RV storage" cost OR rates [region] monthly`
- `"[rv type]" maintenance cost OR winterization OR roof seal annual`

### Features
- `"[make model floorplan]" specifications OR specs OR features layout`
- `"[make model]" weight OR GVWR OR "dry weight" half-ton towable`
- `"[make model]" review walkthrough YouTube`
