# Niche Profiles

Pre-configured settings for common directory niches. Each profile defines: search terms, industry directories, typical subcategories, and expected data availability.

## Restaurants & Food Service

- **Search terms**: restaurant, cafe, eatery, dining, food
- **Industry directories**: TripAdvisor, Yelp, OpenTable, DoorDash, GrubHub, UberEats
- **Subcategories**: Fine dining, Casual dining, Fast casual, Cafes & coffee, Bars & pubs, Bakeries, Food trucks
- **Data availability**: HIGH — ratings and reviews widely available, hours common, menus sometimes extractable
- **Special fields**: Cuisine type, price range ($ to $$$$), dietary options, delivery/takeout, reservation links

## HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical

- **Search terms**: HVAC, heating, cooling, plumber, plumbing, electrician, electrical
- **Industry directories**: Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, ACCA directory
- **Subcategories**: Residential, Commercial, Installation, Repair & maintenance, Emergency service
- **Data availability**: MEDIUM — phone/address common, hours less common, service lists variable
- **Special fields**: License number, emergency availability, service area radius, financing options

## Attorneys & Legal
