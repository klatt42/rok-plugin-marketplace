# Operator Profile & Constraints

## Hardcoded Profile

This plugin is built for a specific operator. All discoveries are filtered through this profile.

### Identity
- Small business owner — $3M specialty contents restoration business (Prism Specialties of DC, MD, VA Metro)
- Deep understanding of small business pain points, especially home services
- Engineering background, computer engagement since 1985
- 2+ years learning AI, AI tools, AI development, micro-SaaS

### Builder Capabilities
- **Primary tool**: Claude Code for development
- **Tech stack**: Next.js, Supabase, Chrome extensions, Python, AI APIs
- **Build speed**: MVP in <4 weeks, solo
- **Launch approach**: Build and launch solo, hire later if traction
- **Q2 2026**: Plan to launch several micro-SaaS tools/apps/extensions

### Philosophy
- "Picks and shovels" — Build tools for business builders, not end-consumer products
- Technology arbitrage — Build what just became possible before the market catches up
- Market arbitrage — Find niches where incumbents haven't adopted AI
- 6-12 month opportunity windows are acceptable
- Social media campaigns to market the tools
- Learn new tech willingly but prefer established stack
