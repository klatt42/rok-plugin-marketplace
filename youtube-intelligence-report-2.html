Validated Tactics

"These tools helped me [problem]" Format

Leverages "being understood > competence" psychology. Shows shared experience + tool list = irresistible. End with contextual question tied to content.

LinkedIn News Commenting

Comment on LinkedIn News stories your ICP cares about. Signals topic expertise to algorithm. Piggybacks existing traffic instead of building from zero.

Contextual CTA (Anti-AI-Slop)

Algorithm trained to detect generic engagement bait. Tie your question/CTA directly to the content. Avoid words the algorithm associates with AI-generated slop.

Visual Posts: Non-LinkedIn-Blue Colors

Use colors that contrast with LinkedIn's blue interface to make posts pop. Either genuine selfie (human emotion) or designed graphic in contrasting colors.

Network Page for Active Users

Free alternative to Sales Navigator ($100+/mo). LinkedIn's Network suggestions show active users. Connect on mobile without custom messages for higher acceptance.

Events as Lead Qualification

Create a LinkedIn Event. Bulk invite connections. Acceptance = hand-raised interest signal. No automation needed, reaches hundreds of targeted people.

Red Flags

Pre-Recorded Video as LinkedIn Live

TOS VIOLATION

Recommends uploading pre-recorded video as "Live" using third-party software. This explicitly violates LinkedIn's Professional Community Policies and risks account restrictions. DO NOT implement.

Heavily Gated Resources

Key tools, prompts, and app names are hidden behind community signup / paywall. The video is partly a funnel for ClientsImpact.

Useful Framework: 3S's + 2F's Content Angles

Scary

Disruptive trends, industry threats, alarming news. Creates urgency and stops the scroll.

Strange

Something unexpected, quirky, contrarian. "Nobody is saying it the way you're saying it." Pattern interrupt.

Sexy

Desirable outcomes and results. "How to make $1M" or "Win a major award." Aspirational, prize-focused.

Familiar

Known faces, brands, or concepts. "3 secrets from Warren Buffett..." Leverages existing trust and recognition.

Free Value

Premium content given away free. "We spent $7K on this research — here it is for free." Must feel like it should cost money.

"With or Without You" Energy

Present CTAs as "it's happening regardless." Zero neediness. "If you want to join, great. If not, totally fine." Confirmed by scarcity psychology research.

Validated Consensus (All 3 Agree)

LinkedIn has fundamentally shifted — massive opportunity for content creators now

All 3 creators confirm: LinkedIn is no longer just for job seekers. It's an active business networking and content platform with less competition than Instagram/YouTube/TikTok.

Profile optimization is essential infrastructure before content creation

All emphasize: professional photo, CTA-driven banner, Featured section for key content, clear tagline. Treat it as a landing page.

Visual content (carousels, images, video) outperforms text-only posts
