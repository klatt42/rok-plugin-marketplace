# Pricing Sources — RV/Camper Specific

## FMV (Fair Market Value) Sources

### Primary: NADA Guides (RV Edition)
- **URL**: nadaguides.com (RV section)
- **Coverage**: Travel trailers, fifth wheels, motorhomes (A/B/C), truck campers, pop-ups, toy haulers
- **Values provided**: Low retail, average retail, high retail, base price
- **Update frequency**: Monthly
- **Notes**: The gold standard for RV values. Used by banks for RV loan approvals. Not the same as NADA vehicle guides.

### JD Power RV Values
- **Coverage**: Major RV brands and models
- **Values provided**: Retail value, trade-in value, private party value
- **Notes**: Good for depreciation projections and brand comparison

### RVTrader Market Data
- **URL**: rvtrader.com
- **Coverage**: Largest RV marketplace, active listings nationwide
- **Values provided**: Current listing prices (not sold prices)
- **Notes**: Best for understanding current asking prices and inventory levels. Does not show actual sale prices.

### PPL Motor Homes (Sold Listings)
- **URL**: pplmotorhomes.com
- **Coverage**: Consignment sales, actual transaction prices
- **Notes**: One of the few sources showing what RVs actually SOLD for, not just listed at. Primarily motorhomes and higher-end travel trailers.

## Inventory Sources

### RVTrader.com
- Largest RV marketplace
- New and used listings
- Dealer and private party
- National coverage with zip code search
- Best first-stop for inventory search

### Camping World / General RV
- Largest dealer chains in the US
- campingworld.com / generalrv.com
- New and used inventory
- Often have show specials and financing promotions

### RVUSA.com
- Dealer listings aggregator
- National coverage
- Good for finding smaller regional dealers

### PPL Motor Homes
- Consignment-based (owner sets price, PPL facilitates)
- Houston, TX location (national shipping available)
- Often better prices than dealer for used
- pplmotorhomes.com

### Facebook Marketplace
- Significant for used RV market
- Private party sales
- No dealer markup but no warranty
- Check for scams — never wire money, always inspect in person

### Manufacturer Dealer Locators
- granddesignrv.com, jayco.com, winnebago.com, etc.
- Find authorized dealers by zip code
- Useful for new inventory specifically

## Dealer Reputation Sources

### Google Reviews
- Most comprehensive review source
- Look for: rating, review count, recent trends, specific complaints
- Red flag: Below 3.5 stars or declining trend

### RV Insider Dealer Reviews
- rvinsider.com
- RV-specific dealer reviews
- Rates sales, service, and parts separately
- Good for understanding service department quality

### Better Business Bureau (BBB)
- bbb.org
- Check accreditation status
- Review complaint history and resolution
- Red flag: Multiple unresolved complaints

### DealerRater
- dealerrater.com
- Some RV dealers listed
- More common for auto dealers but worth checking

## Incentive & Promotion Sources

### Manufacturer Websites
- Current rebates and financing offers
- Show specials (during RV show season Jan-Mar)
- Loyalty and referral programs

### RV Shows
- Major shows: Florida RV Supershow (Tampa, Jan), America's Largest RV Show (Hershey, PA, Sep)
- Regional shows throughout Jan-Mar
- Show-only pricing typically $3,000-$8,000 below normal retail

### Dealer Promotions
- End-of-model-year clearance (varies by brand, usually fall)
- Holiday sales events
- Financing promotions through dealer-affiliated lenders

## Search Query Patterns

### FMV Research
- `"[year] [make] [model]" NADA value OR "book value" site:nadaguides.com`
- `"[year] [make] [model] [floorplan]" price average OR market value`
- `"[make] [model]" depreciation OR "value retention" RV`

### Inventory Search
- `"[year] [make] [model] [floorplan]" for sale [zip] site:rvtrader.com`
- `"[make] [model]" inventory OR "in stock" site:campingworld.com`
- `"[year] [make] [model]" [condition] for sale near [state]`

### Dealer Intelligence
- `"[dealer name]" reviews OR rating Google OR rvinsider`
- `"[dealer name]" complaints OR issues OR "prep fee"`
- `"[dealer name]" BBB OR "better business bureau"`

### Pricing Intelligence
- `"[rv type]" "show special" OR show pricing [year]`
- `"[make]" incentives OR rebates OR financing [month] [year]`
- `"RV negotiation" tips OR strategy OR "how much off MSRP"`
